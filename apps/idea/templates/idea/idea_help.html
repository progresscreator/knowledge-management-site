About iDea - Incentivized Idea Sharing

What is iDea?

iDea is an information market that allows you to post ideas to the Barter community and reap a profit if your ideas take flight. Members of the Barter community rate your ideas, and collaborate around your idea to provide support. If you want to get credit for introducing an idea that causes a positive change in your workplace or university, iDea is the place for you.

Think you're ready to get started? Go ahead and click "Post an idea" to make your first post in iDea!
