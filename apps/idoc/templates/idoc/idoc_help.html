About iDoc - Incentivized Document Sharing

What is iDoc?

iDoc is an information market that allows you to post documents to the Barter community and reap a profit if your postings become popular. Members of the Barter community rate your documents, and pay a one-time fee in order to view what you have posted. If you want to get paid for sharing an important memo, e-mail, or article, iDoc is the market for you.

How does it work?

When you post a document, you can set the price of the download (Points Needed) that each user must pay when accessing the document. These points are credited to your account each time a new user accesses your posting. If your document contains an idea which gives rise to other related media, you receive a kickback for posting the original doc.
